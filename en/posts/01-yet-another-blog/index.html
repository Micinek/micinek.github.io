Yet another blog

November 30, 2024 One-minute read

Why write another tech blog?! Link to heading

I’ve been asking myself for about a year now, during which time I’ve created and deleted this blog several times because I thought I had nothing to say. All things said, videos made, tutorials written, problems solved.

BUT…

I think everyone has a different experience installing software, building hardware, and troubleshooting. We receive information and learn in diferent ways.

I want to share my journey and my own playground outside of work, where I learn new things and get better at my job. This blog will serve as my own self-reflection and documentation. I also hope it will help other people start their journey and build on top of it.

As another challenge, I told myself that even though I have little time to write a blog, I will run the blog bilingually. I miss some instructions and a community in the Czech language.

I hope this blog will help you. Let’s get to the point… Link to heading
